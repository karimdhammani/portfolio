About Me

Creative who codes!

I am a UX/UI designer and product strategist from Toronto, Canada. I am a designer by passion, business strategist by book and entrepreneur by heart. I have recently self-taught myself front-end development and I am on the pathway to become a full-stack designer.

As a design strategist, my process mainly involves finding answers to user’s problems. I like to focus on human-centric design that not only looks good but gets your users to click, buy, sell or act as per the business & product goals. From my experience in working with start-ups, I have gained substantial opertional knowledge of the complete product development life cycle and agile project management.

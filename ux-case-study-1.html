Mobile Banking & Personal Finance

Feature App - UX Case Study

Adding budgeting and personal finance management features to solve user problems of over spending & keeping track of transactions.

Project Overview

How it began

It all started with a few unwanted expenses I made while upgrading my workstation. One fine day after making series of bad shopping choices I was looking at my transactions trying to keep track of my budget and I had to switch between three apps i.e., notion, my bank app and calculator. The experience of switching between apps was very frustrating and that‚Äôs when the lightning stroke. So, this case study is focused on UX design of a Banking concept app with features that will enable users to manage their personal finance from their mobile banking app. My concept idea is to bridge the gap between banking & personal finance apps

The Problem

One major problem with mobile banking apps that I am trying to shed some light on is the feature set for managing personal finances. There are several personal finance apps available for the users but there is not any app that lets users use their transaction history to map that particular transaction into a specific category.

The solution

The solution is simple i.e., enable the users with features in their bank app to create budget categories, add spending limits to the categories and the ability to map their transactions into specific budget category. The users can also be provided with analytics of their spending patterns based on various metrics that this feature can study and analyse.

Idea Validation

To dig deeper into the gap of mobile banking and personal finance management I researched online reports and statistics from research databases. After a gathering all my research findings and synthesising my learning outcomes, My hypothesis was getting proved.

Hypothesis Validation

In general, the hypothesis made at the beginning of the research was proved: People have trouble managing their finances. There exists a gap between personal finance management apps and user‚Äôs mobile banking app. To maintain good financial health and to keep a habit of managing personal finance users have to use multiple tools alongside their banking app. Hence, the features of personal finance management app integrated into mobile banking would be beneficial for the users.

Research Synthesis

In this stage I gathered my research findings and analyzed the result to create a validated user persona, user journey map, and flowchart. The key takeaways that I got from the research are:

Common user problems

  • Difficulty in managing the habit of keeping budgets.

  • Forgetting recurring bill payments.

  • Over expenditure on shopping.

  • Unable to save and achieve financial & life goals.

User Goals

  • Quickly view balance and transactions.

  • Create budget categories with spending limits.

  • Auto deduct expenses from budget categories.

  • Reminders and reports on spending limits.

Prototype & Interactions

Swipeable Cards on Home Screen

The swipeable realistic credit card design adds familiarity and quick access to debit cards, credit cards, payment cards or wallet. On tapping the card the card specific transactions will appear.

Quickly Transfer Money

Using the shortcut on the home screen below the cards, the users can transfer money to other users or between accounts quickly in less than 5 taps on the screen.

Create Custom Budgets

The create budgets feature is also very accessible from the home as well as through the second menu option of 'My Personal finances' which is dedicated menu for the feature set of personal finance management.

Add Expenses to Budgets

Users can quickly select transactions from the list to mapp it to the specific budget category that they want the expense to be deducted from. This will save time and manual inputs from the users.

Goals Review & Learnings

According to me successful user experience is connecting business goals to customer motivations and creating solutions that achieve good outcomes for business while solving the needs of the customer. Howver the approach companies need to take should be focused on solving user problems which can ultimately lead the company to growth and achievement of business goals.

In this concept app my main objective was to solve a very common problem of personal finance management. As this pandemic has helped us realise is how important it is to know how to manage our personal finances and how to take better decisions thinking about the future. We all need a better handle of our money management.

Business Goals

  • To provide more value to the customers by introducing a new feature in the mobile banking app.

  • To attract specific target market that are interested in personal finance management through an app.

  • To gain more insights of the users with the purpose of launching customer centric and personalised banking products based on user needs.

  • To gain the market share and jump on the Neo Bank Bandwagon.

  • To promote personal finance management through the banking app additional to banking services.

User Goals

  • Have users create accounts and budget categories.

  • Give them access to all banking needs in single tap.

  • Enable them to manage, add or deduct income & expenses from budget categories.

  • Encourage customers to be aware of their personal finances.

  • Provide actionable insights to users to take better decisions.

  • To bridge the gap between personal finance management apps and the mobile banking app.
